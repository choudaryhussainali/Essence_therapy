What to expect

A path built around safety, trust, and your pace

Every person's journey is different, so therapy is personalised. You're always in control of how far and how fast we go.

1

Safety first

We begin by establishing stability, trust, and a sense of safety — long before exploring difficult memories.

2

Build your tools

You'll learn grounding, mindfulness, and coping skills to steady anxiety and tension as we go.

3

Process gently

When you're ready, we gently work through the experiences and beliefs that trauma left behind.

4

Find resilience

You begin to rewrite your story — finding meaning, strength, and a renewed sense of yourself.

  • You experience ongoing flashbacks or nightmares, even years after the event.
